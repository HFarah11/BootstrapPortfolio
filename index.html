About Me

Hey All! My name is Harun! I am a young professional from Toronto, Ontario. I am currently enrolled at the Uoft Continued Studies faculty where I am registered in a Programming BootCamp. This is my bootstrap recreation of my initial HTML + CSS assignment.

It was one of my greatest accomplishments being able to graduate.

I plan to use the skills I learn in my coding bootcamp as a pivot. I want to continue to build upon what I completed in my bachelor's, psychology, and pair it with my new skills, coding, to enter further my professional career.
